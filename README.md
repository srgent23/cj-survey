# JourneyIQ | B&P Journey Readiness Report

A structured CX maturity assessment for utility billing and payments leaders. 36 questions, 6 dimensions, instant scored report.

---

## What It Does

You answer 36 questions across six dimensions of the Billing & Payments customer journey. The tool scores your responses, benchmarks them against Chartwell 2024 industry data, and generates a report with a radar chart, priority stack, and 30/60/90 day roadmap.

Built for CX Directors benchmarking before a peer conversation. No backend, no submission, no account required. Everything runs in the browser.

---

## The Six Dimensions

| | Dimension | Core Question |
|---|---|---|
| D1 | VoC Coverage | Do you hear from customers at the moments that matter? |
| D2 | Signal-to-Action Pipeline | When you get a signal, does anything happen? |
| D3 | Digital Payment Execution | Are your digital channels actually working? |
| D4 | Proactive Communication | Are you reaching customers before they call? |
| D5 | CX Governance | Does the work have a home? |
| D6 | Operational Enablement | Can your org reliably deliver the experience at scale? |
